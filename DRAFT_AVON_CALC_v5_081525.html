Notes
  1. The existing operating referendum was originally approved for a rate not to exceed $0.3536 in May 2018 and set to expire in 2026. The School Corporation has proposed a lower tax rate of $0.3300, representing a $0.0236 reduction in the total referendum tax rate. The proposed continuation of the school operating referendum with a maximum tax rate of $0.3300 is limited to an eight (8) year term beginning in 2026 through and including 2033. The purpose of the operating referendum is for funding the retention and attraction of teachers, educational staff, and other support staff, and funding educationally related programs.
  2. Real property assessments are adjusted annually due to changes in market conditions. Taxpayer liability will change in relation to changes in parcel gross assessed value.
  3. Homestead parcels are assumed to have homestead and supplemental homestead deductions according to I.C. 6-1.1-20.6-7.7 and a Department of Local Government Finance memo dated June 12, 2025.
  4. Other residential, rental, agricultural, and long-term care properties are assumed to receive a standard deductions according to I.C. 6-1.1-12-47 and a Department of Local Government Finance memo dated June 12, 2025.
  5. Senate Enrolled Act 1 (SEA1) 2025 was signed into law on April 15, 2025, and includes several changes impacting property taxes to be phased in through 2031. SEA1 increases homestead deductions and provides a supplemental homestead credit of the lesser of $300 or 10% of the property tax bill. SEA1 adds deductions for properties subject to the 2% tax cap, increases the exemption threshold for business personal property and eliminates the 30% floor for new business personal property. This calculator does not factor in the supplemental homestead deduction of the lesser of $300 or 10% of the property tax bill.
